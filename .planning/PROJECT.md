# Project: Personal House & Pet Sitter Website

## Vision

A professional, conversion-focused website for a personal house and pet sitter service provider in Gran Canaria. The website serves as a sales funnel to attract and convert potential clients who need pet care and house sitting services while they're away.

## Problem Statement

Pet owners and homeowners in Gran Canaria need a trustworthy, reliable individual to care for their pets and home while traveling. They want to see credentials, reviews, and clear service offerings before making contact. They need to easily discover the service through search (especially local SEO for Gran Canaria) and book appointments seamlessly.

## Solution

A professional website that:
- Establishes trust and credibility through credentials, testimonials, and transparency
- Clearly communicates services and pricing
- Provides an easy path to contact and book via Calendly integration
- Showcases testimonials and credentials
- Is optimized for local SEO and AI search discovery in Gran Canaria
- Converts visitors into clients through a clear sales funnel

## Target Audience

**Primary Persona 1: Pet Owner Planning Travel**
- Age: 30-60
- Needs: Reliable pet care while away (dogs, cats, or other pets)
- Concerns: Trust, reliability, pet safety, home security
- Decision factors: Reviews, credentials, availability, pricing
- Location: Gran Canaria (expats, tourists, locals)

**Primary Persona 2: Homeowner Needing House Care**
- Age: 35-65
- Needs: House maintenance, security, plant care while traveling
- Concerns: Home security, property maintenance, trustworthiness
- Decision factors: Insurance, background checks, experience
- Location: Gran Canaria

## Success Criteria

### Launch Success
- Website is live and accessible
- All core features functional
- Mobile-responsive
- Calendly booking iframe working correctly
- Payment processing via Stripe functional
- Contact form working
- Professional appearance

### Business Success (3-6 months)
- 10+ inquiries per month
- 3-5 bookings per month
- Positive client feedback
- Low bounce rate (<50%)
- High time on site (2+ minutes)

### SEO Success (3-6 months)
- Ranking in top 10 for "pet sitter Gran Canaria"
- Ranking in top 10 for "house sitter Gran Canaria"
- 10+ Google reviews with 4.5+ star average
- 100+ organic visitors per month
- 30%+ of traffic from organic search
- 20%+ of bookings from organic search

## Technical Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 16 (App Router)
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **UI Components**: shadcn/ui
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Form Handling**: React Hook Form + Zod validation
- **Form Submission**: Next.js Server Actions
- **Email Service**: Resend (or SendGrid for free tier)
- **Spam Protection**: hCaptcha (or reCAPTCHA v3)
- **Booking System**: Calendly Professional with embedded iframe + native Stripe payments
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe integration through Calendly (built-in)
- **Hosting**: Vercel (recommended) or Netlify
- **SEO**: Comprehensive local SEO and AI-ready optimization for Gran Canaria

## Key Constraints

- Must be optimized for Gran Canaria local SEO
- Must work seamlessly on mobile devices
- Must integrate with Calendly for booking
- Must collect payments via Stripe
- Must be AI-search ready (structured data, semantic HTML)
- Must be fast loading (< 3 seconds)
- Must be accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA compliance)

## Project Context

This is a personal service website, not a platform. The focus is on:
1. Building trust and credibility
2. Clear communication of services
3. Easy booking process
4. Local discoverability in Gran Canaria
5. Conversion optimization
